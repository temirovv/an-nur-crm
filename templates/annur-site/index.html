Yes, many of our teachers are native English speakers. We also have highly qualified local teachers who are fluent in English and have extensive teaching experience.

Each course typically lasts 3 months, with classes held twice a week. However, we also offer intensive courses that can be completed in 6 weeks.

We offer courses for all levels, from beginner (A1) to advanced (C2). Our programs are designed to meet the needs of students at any stage of their language learning journey.

Our classes usually have 8 to 12 students. We believe small class sizes allow for more personalized instruction and better interaction between students and teachers.

You can register online through our website, or visit our center in person. For online registration, simply fill out the form on our registration page, and our staff will contact you to confirm your enrollment.

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit/debit card, and cash. You can choose to pay for the entire course upfront or in monthly installments.

Yes, we offer both online and in-person classes. Our online classes are conducted via Zoom, with the same high-quality instruction as our in-person courses.

We provide all necessary learning materials, including textbooks and online resources. You will need access to a computer or tablet for online classes.

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not happy with the course within the first two weeks, you can request a full refund.
